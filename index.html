Biography

Julian Graham is a dynamic actor known for a variety of roles in TV and film. Born in the UK, Julian has dual citizenship in the UK and USA. He’s a former Police Officer and ex-amateur champion boxer with a keen interest in martial arts, classic cars and riding motorcycles.
