Biography

Julian Graham is a dynamic actor known for a variety of roles in TV and film. He has recurred on shows such as The Catch (ABC) and as a recurring guest on Dynasty (CW/Netflix). He has worked closely with stars such as JK Simmons on Counterpart (Starz), the late great Miguel Ferrer in NCIS LA (CBS) and Ryan Phillippe on Shooter (USA Network). Recently he guest starred in Tyler Perry’s All the Queen’s Men (BET) working opposite Eva Marcille.

Born in the UK, Julian has dual citizenship in the UK and USA. He’s a former Police Officer and ex-amateur champion boxer with a keen interest in martial arts, classic cars and riding motorcycles.
